Welcome to Brantley Bank & Trust Co.

Established in 1946, Brantley Bank & Trust Company has a rich tradition of service. As an independent, locally owned institution our primary interest is the financial welfare of our neighbors. All of our Bank Officers, Directors and employees have roots deep in the community. You'll find us there with you in the schools, in church or at the ballgame.

We also take great pride in the quality and variety of products we have to offer. Despite our lack of size we are committed to offering our customers a "State of the art" selection of products and services.

At either our main office in Brantley or our branches in Ramer or Pike Road, you'll always be greeted by people who know you and more importantly, care about you.
